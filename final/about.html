Hi! I'm a freelance designer and general office suite expert located in Baltimore, MD. Don't let that scare you though. I'm available for all kinds of remote work.


But first, a little about myself. My career as been varied and I have traveled to multiple locations. I designed my first website in 1995 while I was in high school, but then took a long detour from professional design through college, into the U.S. Navy, and then through a variety of nuclear and electrical operation jobs.


I have traveled North America, Europe and the Middle East, mainly in pursuit of history, food, and women's soccer games.


Through all this though I have been gaining a variety of skills that make me extremely useful to small businesses. I have performed a wide variety of tasks throughout my career ranging from system testing to server upkeep and maintenance. I earned an M.B.A. during my Navy time and then went back and got a degree in Accounting. After that, I fully utilized my GI bill while working full time to gain a computer science degree and a digital media & web technology degree.


All this means that I am uniquely qualified to be a one stop shop for you small business design needs. Whether you need logos and menus, your first website, or a hand setting up a simple accounting database, I can be of assistance for all your digital needs at a price that you can afford.
